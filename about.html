About Wine Valet

At first glance, Wine Valet is a tech company that is developing an algorithm to personalise wine recommendations. However, WHY Wine Valet exists is to share stories of grit and passion with the world. Many of the wineries we work with are owned by families who pursue the dream of winemaking, which is a tough gig most of the time. There are floods, and drought, and frost, and financial troubles, yet year after year these families prune their vines, wait for the grapes to grow, pick, crush and make a unique product vintage on vintage. Wine Valet exists to share the passion of winemaking with the world.
